Japanglish

Japanglish: Trip to Tokyo is an educative visual novel that teaches Japanglish words to English speakers. As any other visual novel, our game mainly focuses on a story and dialogues. As the story progresses, a player gets tested on a variety of Japanglish words that naturally appear in a conversation. The happiness metre shows how successful you are at guessing their meanings. Should the hearts drop to zero, it's a game over and you have to start again. The game has several locations, each consisting of a unique set of test vocabulary connected to a certain place such as a train station, school, café, restaurant, shopping centre and home.

Used technologies: CSS animations, React
